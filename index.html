The Problem

Most mid-market brands are stranded.

Big agencies are priced for the Fortune 500. Freelancers cannot carry the load. So content drifts. Visual identity erodes. Voice splinters across channels. Footage gets re-shot because nobody can find what already exists. The brand becomes whatever the last junior staffer published.

The retainer fixes the operating layer, not the surface. A senior team runs the system. The brand stops drifting.

The Retainer

A fractional, senior content operations capability.

The Brand Operations Retainer is built around five named, productized services. Each is a discrete capability with its own definition, deliverables, and standard. Together they form a content operating system.

01

Story Film

The cinematic narrative centerpiece. AI-generated imagery, the founder's actual recorded voice, no likeness rendering. Delivered in every format — 16:9, 9:16, 1:1. One per cadence depending on tier. The marketing flagship every month or quarter.

02

Brand Atlas

The canonical living document defining brand voice, color, typography, message architecture, prohibited language, and tone rules. Established in implementation, maintained through the retainer, enforced across every deliverable. The Atlas keeps the brand internally consistent at scale.

03

Channel Cadence

Predictable multi-channel content production on a weekly or daily rhythm. LinkedIn, YouTube, Instagram — selected by tier. Each long-form unit is cut into shorts, snippets, and posts. One production cycle generates a week of content. No scrambling, no last-minute drafting.

04

Campaign Architecture

Strategic content sprints for the moments that matter. Product launches, funding rounds, executive hires, partnership announcements. We design the multi-asset campaign blueprint and coordinate execution across every channel in advance. The moment lands.

05

Semantic Bridge

On-premise AI infrastructure for asset retrieval. The production team natural-language-searches the brand's entire footage and graphics archive. Anything retrieved in seconds. Never re-shoot what already exists. Hardware at cost, software licensed by VDO.
